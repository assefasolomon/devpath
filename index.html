Frequently asked questions
How do I pay? ▾
After registering, you will receive a unique reference code. Transfer 599 ETB to our Telebirr or CBE account using that code as your transfer note. Then submit your transaction ID on our site and we verify within 1–2 hours.
Which payment methods are accepted? ▾
We accept Telebirr, CBE (Commercial Bank of Ethiopia), Awash Bank, and other Ethiopian bank transfers. No international cards or PayPal required.
How long until I get access? ▾
We verify payments within 1–2 hours during business hours (8am–8pm). Once verified you get instant access to all courses.
Is this a one-time payment? ▾
Yes — pay once and access everything forever. No subscriptions, no hidden fees. Future content additions are also included at no extra cost.
What if I already paid but don't have access? ▾
Log in to your account and check the status on the payment page. If your payment was submitted but not yet verified, it means we are still checking. If it has been more than 2 hours contact us directly.
